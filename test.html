Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get a 5G connection on my iPhone 12?
All iPhone 12 and iPhone 12 Pro models are 5G capable. For 5G access, you need to activate your iPhone with a carrier that
offers 5G service. Some carriers may also require a 5G service plan or a new SIM. Contact your carrier for more
information. To learn more, visit https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT204039
